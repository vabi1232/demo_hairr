Dryers

  • Hair dryer is an extremely important beauty item when it not only helps to dry hair quickly but also helps us to create beautiful hairstyles.
    Although useful, but not everyone knows how to use them effectively, this article will guide you to properly care for your hair with a hairdryer.
    1. Do not dry your hair right after washing your hair.
    2. Dry hair at a moderate temperature, choose a cool drying mode (if any).
    3. Continuous reverse airflow.
    4. Do not shake the dryer.
    5. Hold the machine in the correct position, do not cover the dryer radiator.

Steamers

  • Steaming hair oil (also known as: hair steaming, hair steaming, hair recovery) is the process of using the temperature and moisture from the steam in combination with mist to increase the effectiveness of hair conditioning. The effect of steaming hair is to help open hair follicles, the cuticles on the hair shaft, thereby allowing conditioner, conditioner, and hair oil to penetrate deeply into each hair. Therefore, absorbing hair oil will allow your hair to better absorb moisture and nutrients from the nourishing oil, thereby helping to support the health of your hair. In addition, steaming oil also helps to encourage blood circulation in your scalp, contributing to your hair health, growing longer and thicker.
    1. Steaming hair oil will help increase the hair's moisture content.
    2. Hair oil absorption helps hair absorb nutrients from the nourishing oil better.
    3. Steaming oil helps blood circulation in your scalp better, hair is healthier.
    4. Steaming hair oil will expand your hair to make it easier for nutrients from the outside to get into your hair.
